Monthly Farming Tips

June Farming Tips

Crop Management

  • Complete sowing of kharif crops like paddy, maize, and pulses
  • Apply first dose of fertilizer to standing crops
  • Monitor for early pest infestations in young crops

Water Management

  • Prepare water harvesting structures before monsoon intensifies
  • Clean irrigation channels and check for blockages
  • Install rainwater collection systems if not already in place

Pest Control

  • Watch for increased pest activity due to humidity
  • Apply preventive measures for common monsoon pests
  • Use pheromone traps to monitor pest populations

July Farming Tips

Crop Management

  • Complete any remaining kharif sowing operations
  • Apply second dose of fertilizer to early-sown crops
  • Thin overcrowded crop stands to optimize growth

Water Management

  • Ensure proper drainage in fields during heavy rainfall
  • Monitor soil moisture levels regularly
  • Prepare contingency plans for flood-prone areas

Pest Control

  • Watch for fungal diseases due to high humidity
  • Apply appropriate fungicides if symptoms appear
  • Monitor for stem borers in rice and maize
